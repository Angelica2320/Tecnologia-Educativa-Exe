Do and Does are auxiliary verbs used in the present simple tense to make
affirmative sentences, negative sentences and questions.

We use DO with:
I, You, We, They

We use DOES with:
He, She, It

AFFIRMATIVE
I do like apples.
She does like apples.

NEGATIVE
I do not like milk.
He does not like milk.

QUESTIONS
Do you like pizza?
Does she like pizza?
